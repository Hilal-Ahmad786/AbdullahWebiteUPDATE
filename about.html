About CSYGA

Discover about us, our mission and vision to empower youth in global diplomacy and international affairs.

About Us

The Center for Strategic Youth and Global Affairs (CSYGA) is a youth-driven, impact-oriented initiative that empowers young people through strategic education, cross-cultural dialogue, and high-level global engagement.

By 2030, we aim to establish a global network of 10,000 youth diplomats actively engaged in shaping international policy across all continents.

We provide platforms for youth to participate in shaping international affairs, fostering cooperation, and promoting peace and sustainable development. Through diplomacy training, think tanks, exchange programs, and forums, we cultivate strategic leadership across borders.

Our Mission

At CSYGA, we believe in the transformative power of youth leadership in shaping the future of global diplomacy. Our mission is to equip young leaders with the knowledge, skills, and networks necessary to become effective agents of change in international affairs.

Through innovative programs and strategic partnerships, we create platforms for meaningful youth participation in diplomatic processes, policy development, and cross-cultural dialogue.

We are committed to building bridges between generations and cultures, fostering mutual understanding, and developing solutions to the world's most pressing challenges.

Our Vision

We envision a world where young people are not just participants but leaders in global governance, where their perspectives are integral to diplomatic processes, and where intergenerational collaboration drives sustainable solutions for global challenges.

By 2030, we aim to establish a global network of 10,000 youth diplomats actively engaged in shaping international policy across all continents.

Our vision includes creating institutional pathways for youth participation in diplomatic missions, international organizations, and multilateral negotiations.
